About our services

Visa Guidance for International Students

Navigating the visa process is a crucial step in your journey to studying in the United States. At Project ScholaMundi, we provide comprehensive guidance to ensure a smooth and successful visa application process. Here’s how we help you every step of the way:


Initial Consultation:

    Sign Up:

  • Start by filling out our online registration form with your basic information and details about your college admission status.

    • Meet Your Counselor:

  • Once registered, you’ll be assigned a dedicated counselor who will arrange an initial meeting to discuss your visa needs and answer any questions you may have.

Understanding Visa Requirements:

    Visa Types:

  • We’ll explain the different types of student visas and help you determine which one is right for you.

    • Eligibility Criteria:

  • Your counselor will review the eligibility criteria for your chosen visa type and ensure you meet all requirements.

Document Preparation:

    Required Documents:

  • We’ll provide a detailed checklist of all necessary documents, including your I-20 or DS-2019 form, passport, financial statements, and admission letter.

    • Document Review:

  • Your counselor will review each document to ensure accuracy and completeness.

Financial Documentation:

    Proof of Funds:

  • Guidance on preparing and presenting financial documents to demonstrate your ability to cover tuition and living expenses.

    • Affidavits and Support Letters:

  • Assistance with obtaining and organizing any additional financial support documents, such as affidavits of support from sponsors.






Visa Application Process:

    SEVIS Fee Payment:

  • Instructions on paying the SEVIS I-901 fee and obtaining the receipt.

    • DS-160 Form:

  • Step-by-step guidance on completing the DS-160 online visa application form.

Scheduling Your Visa Interview:

    Appointment Booking:

  • Assistance with booking your visa interview appointment at the nearest US embassy or consulate.

    • Interview Preparation:

  • Tips and practice sessions to help you prepare for the visa interview, including common questions and how to present yourself confidently.

Attending Your Visa Interview:

    What to Expect:

  • Detailed information on what to expect during your visa interview, including required documents and interview procedures.

    • Post-Interview Guidance:

  • Advice on next steps after your interview, including tracking your visa application status and what to do if additional documents are requested.

Pre-Departure Support:

    Travel Arrangements:

  • Guidance on booking your flights and planning your travel to the United States.

    • Arrival Checklist:

  • A comprehensive checklist of tasks to complete upon arrival, such as attending orientation, registering with your school, and obtaining necessary permits.
