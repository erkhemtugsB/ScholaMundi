About Schola Mundi

Welcome to ScholaMundi

Project ScholaMundi is a non-profit that does the sole function of providing free consultation services to underrepresented student communities from all over the world to pursue college education in the United States. Our goal is to promote US education to academically qualified students from nations around the world, ensuring that they are admitted to top-tier US institutions and are awarded the maximum receipt of scholarship they deserve. Our services are fully free and centered on the higher education welfare of the public.
